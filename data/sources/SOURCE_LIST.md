# Data Sources for Ministry AI Governance Assistant

**Goal:** Curate 15-20 high-quality, public sources on AI ethics, church technology, and ministry best practices.

**Status:** Sources Collected

---

## Collection Criteria

**Must Have:**
- Publicly accessible (no paywalls)
- Authoritative source (reputable organization/author)
- Relevant to: AI ethics, church tech, ministry practices, privacy
- Recent (prefer 2022-2025 content)
- Clear licensing (can be used for educational purposes)

**Avoid:**
- Opinion pieces without citations
- Marketing/sales content
- Paywalled academic papers
- Content requiring authentication
